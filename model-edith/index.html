Edith Bangura is a Sierra Leonean-born, Ghana-based model whose elegance, confidence, and versatility have made her a rising star in the fashion industry. A graduate of the prestigious 5-Star Modelling College in Ghana, Edith brings both professionalism and artistry to every runway she graces and every camera she faces.

From runway shows to editorial spreads, commercial campaigns, and photo shoots, Edith has proven herself as a dynamic force—commanding attention with her striking presence and leaving lasting impressions with her refined style. Her journey across countless fashion shows has earned her a strong reputation among designers, photographers, and industry leaders alike.

Driven, passionate, and committed to excellence, Edith Bangura embodies the future of African fashion—where talent meets resilience, and beauty meets purpose.

“Modelling is more than wearing fashion—it’s about telling stories, embodying creativity, and inspiring confidence. Every runway I walk, every campaign I represent, and every editorial I bring to life is a chance to celebrate beauty, culture, and individuality.”

I am Edith Bangura, a Sierra Leonean model based in Ghana, and my journey has been shaped by passion, discipline, and resilience. From graduating at 5-Star Modelling College in Ghana to walking in numerous shows and collaborating with visionary creatives, I have built a reputation for professionalism and versatility.

My mission is to use modelling not only as a career but as a platform—to empower, to inspire, and to showcase the richness of African talent on the global stage. With every step, I strive to merge elegance with authenticity, creating moments that resonate far beyond the runway.

I sincerely thank 5-Star Modelling College for shaping my journey. Your guidance, discipline, and inspiration built my confidence and professionalism. Every runway I walk reflects your impact. I am proud to represent your excellence in the fashion industry.
